Tofu Scramble

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

The best tofu scramble! Tofu!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 16-ounce block firm tofu
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons non-dairy milk, unsweetened and unflavored

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat then mash the tofu block right in the pan. Cook, stirring frequently, for 3-4 minutes until water from the tofu is mostly gone.
  2. Add nutritional yeast, salt, tumeric, and garlic powder. Cook and stir for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour non-dairy milk into pan and stir to mix. Serve immediately with sliced avocado, hot sauce, parsley, steamed kale, toast, or any other breakfast item.
